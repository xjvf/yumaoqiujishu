10 Exercise Motivation Quotes

Posted on 2016-06-27 | Edited on 2018-05-29
  1. A year from now you’ll wish you had started today.

  2. Wow, I really regret that workout.

  3. You don’t have to be great to start, but you have to start to be great.

  4. Exercise…you’ll NEVER regret doing it, you’ll ALWAYS regret not doing it, so go do it…NOW

  5. You can feel sore tomorrow or you can feel sorry tomorrow. You choose.

  6. Yesterday you said tomorrow. Just do it.

  7. Be stronger than you excuses.

  8. Take care of your body. It’s the only place you have to live in.

  9. You’re only one workout away form a good mood.

  10. If comfort were the goal, no one in their right mind would wake up before the sun and put their body through pain. But it’s not comfort we’re after. It’s progress. However small.
